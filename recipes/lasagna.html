Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parm. Reg. Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Basil
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Soak lasagna noodles in water
  2. Layer bottom of 8"x12" glass baking dish with lasagna noodles
  3. Add layer of cheese, basil, spinach, and tomato sauce
  4. Repeat until the top of the dish has been reached
  5. Spread tomato sauce on top layer
  6. Sprinkle remainder of cheese on top
  7. Bake at 350 deg. for 1 hour
