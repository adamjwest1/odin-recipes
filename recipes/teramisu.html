Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Lady Fingers
  • Espresso
  • Marscapone Cheese
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Soak lady fingers in espresso
  2. Place a layer of soaked lady fingers in a 8"x8" baking dish
  3. Mix sugar, heavy cream, and marscapone cheese
  4. Spread a layer of marscapone cheese mix on top of lady finger
  5. Repeat until layers reach the top of the dish
  6. Dust the top layer with cocoa powder
